#### `inv(input: any[] | Mat) : any[]`

**param** `input` a 2d matrix. Should be square and invertible. 

**returns:** `any[]` - The matrix that is the inverse of `input` i.e. `input * input^-1 = I` where `I` is the identity matrix. (recall identity matrix is 1's on the diagonal and 0's elsewhere)
