#### `bounds(dataIn: any[] | Mat | Tensor, dim: number) : Mat | any[]`

**param** `dataIn` - series of `number`s, an `Array`, matrix/`Mat` or `Tensor` to get the min and max of

**param** `dim` (optional, default=0), '0'-(bound of all), '1'-(bound of column), '2'-(bound of row)

**returns:** `Mat | any[]` - 1-D array or 2-D matrix - [min, max] or [[min1, min2], [max1, max2]], [[min1, max1], [min2, max2]]

This function finds the minimum and maximum value of the given `dataIn`, which is a series of `number`s, an `Array`, a matrix/`Mat` or `Tensor`. 

For 2-D `Arrays`/`Mat` objects, you can use the `dim` parameter to determine if you get the bounds of all values or according to a row or column.
